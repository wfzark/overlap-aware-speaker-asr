# Frontier Reading: Causal & Internal-State Whisper Hallucination Detection (2024-2026)

Literature grounding for the causal & internal-state hallucination probe (`src/causal_hallucination_probe.py`, issue #855). Generated by a 6-agent deep-research sweep (5 parallel finders + synthesis); every paper is listed, claims are paraphrased, no source text reproduced.

## Search Methodology

**Databases searched:** Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar, ACL Anthology, arXiv (cs.CL, eess.AS)

**Query terms:**
- "Whisper hallucination detection" + "internal state" / "decoder" / "confidence"
- "speech recognition hallucination" + "early detection" / "streaming" / "causal"
- "ASR hallucination" + "token-level" / "trajectory" / "attractor"
- "Whisper" + "self-attention" / "rank collapse" / "degeneracy"

**Date range:** January 2024 – June 2026

**Inclusion criteria:** Peer-reviewed venues (ACL, Interspeech, ICASSP, NeurIPS, OpenReview) or arXiv preprints with >5 citations or direct relevance to Whisper hallucination mechanisms.

**Exclusion criteria:** General ASR papers without hallucination focus; non-English-only studies; papers on training/fine-tuning without mechanistic analysis.

**Agents:** 5 parallel search agents with different query strategies + 1 synthesis agent for deduplication and verdict assignment.

**Limitations:** The search is not exhaustive — it covers the most visible 2024–2026 work but may miss recent arXiv preprints or workshop papers. The token-id lock-in novelty claim is based on this search; a negative result (someone else already did it) is possible.

## 1. TL;DR — does the literature support or refute each hypothesis?

- **(a) Confident-loop mechanism — SUPPORTED, reduced headline novelty.** "Hallucination = HIGH-confidence loop, not confidence collapse" is now well-established for Whisper on silence/OOD. Aparin et al. (2026): the built-in filter fails because hallucinations carry elevated `avg_logprob`. Waldendorf et al. (ACL 2026): uncertainty metrics fail exactly in the clean/low-entropy regime. Calm-Whisper localizes it to a few decoder self-attention heads; Viakhirev et al. formalize a rank-collapse attractor. The decoupling is **named** — we extend it, we do not discover it.
- **(b) Early internal-state detection beats output compression-ratio — PARTIALLY SUPPORTED, novelty at risk.** Encoder activations / SAE latents are linearly separable pre-loop (Aparin). Per-token trajectory detection has direct prior art (Corpataux et al., "Local Confidence Drop"). **The token-id repetition lock-in trip-wire appears unoccupied** — our sharpest novel slot.
- **(c) Causal deployment gap — OPEN / NOVEL.** No 2024-2026 paper quantifies an offline guard-gated ASR router's gain decay under prefix forcing. Whisper-CD (token-level contrastive decoding) **refutes the strongest form** ("causal loses most gain") — so we scope to *output-metric gating* losing power causally, while *decode-time intervention* survives.
- **Cross-cutting caution (Waldendorf):** internal-state detectors are model/task-specific and do NOT transfer across ASR/S2TT. A Whisper probe must be validated on Whisper; "generalizes to deployment" is a hypothesis to test.
- **The separation-tax crossover is NOT novel** — Sato et al. (Interspeech 2021) established separation-hurts-below-a-crossover with an SIR/SNR-gated router. Our delta must be the Whisper-specific heavy hallucination tail + mechanism + causal detection.

## 2. Per-hypothesis verdicts

### (a) Confident loop / encoder-decoder decoupling
FOR: Aparin (filter fails on confident hallucinations; encoder/decoder misaligned); Waldendorf (uncertainty metrics weak where hallucinations are rare and confident; attention features win +0.23 PR-AUC); Calm-Whisper (3/20 heads drive >75% of non-speech hallucinations); Viakhirev (Compression-Seeking Attractor, self-attention rank collapse).
AGAINST/novelty-risk: decoupling is named in Aparin; `no_speech_prob` coupling is a known engineering detail; rank collapse attributed mainly to self-attention in larger models; Frieske & Shi (2024) already frame ASR hallucinations as fluent, probable, low-entropy.
**Verdict: SUPPORTED, reduced novelty.** Frame as extending the confident-attractor line to the separation-tax oracle-silence regime.

### (b) Early internal-state detection
FOR: Aparin (encoder/SAE separability increases toward deeper layers, pre-loop); Corpataux (per-token Local Confidence Drop, 0.64 AP on FLEURS — direct prior art); compression-ratio is empirically late (jumps 1.4→12 only at loop onset).
AGAINST/novelty-risk: per-token trajectory detection already done (Corpataux); encoder separability overlaps our framing; detectors are task/model-specific.
**Verdict: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED.** Novel slot = **token-id repetition lock-in as an early causal trip-wire** (unoccupied); Corpataux/Aparin are the bedrock.

### (c) Causal deployability gap
FOR: compression-ratio structurally late/non-causal; Viakhiref (token logprobs fail to predict onset because model stays locally confident while decoupled); Huo et al. (overconfident wrong tokens 10-20% at low SNR, non-monotonic); abstention work is in vision-language/medical QA, not streaming ASR.
AGAINST/novelty-risk: Whisper-CD (training-free token-level contrastive decoding, up to 24.3pp WER gain) refutes the strongest form — causal *intervention* keeps gain.
**Verdict: OPEN/NOVEL, must scope.** Quantifying offline guard-gated router gain decay under prefix forcing is unpublished for ASR. Frame as "output-metric gating vs internal-state/decode-time gating under prefix forcing"; concede Whisper-CD.

## 3. Key papers (paraphrased, cite-don't-claim)

- **Aparin et al. (2026)** — Whisper's filter fails on confident hallucinations; encoder/SAE latents linearly separable pre-loop; steering cuts hallucination 86.9%→27.3%. https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07473
- **Waldendorf et al. (ACL 2026 Findings)** — Uncertainty metrics fail on clean/confident hallucinations; attention-collapse-to-early-frames signature; detectors are task/model-specific. https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19565
- **Corpataux et al. (OpenReview 2026, "Local Confidence Contrasts")** — Per-token Local Confidence Drop detects confident hallucinations as local discontinuities; direct prior art for trajectory detection. https://openreview.net/forum?id=0YJVScMjSs
- **Wang et al., Calm-Whisper (Interspeech 2025)** — 3/20 decoder heads cause >75% of non-speech hallucinations; head-mask fine-tune cuts it >80% at <0.1% WER. https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.12969
- **Viakhirev et al. (2026, "From Dispersion to Attraction")** — Compression-Seeking Attractor with self-attention rank collapse decoupling decoder from acoustics. https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08591
- **Ahn et al., Whisper-CD (Interspeech 2026)** — Training-free token-level contrastive decoding (silence/noise/shift negatives); up to 24.3pp WER gain. https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06193
- **Koenecke et al., Careless Whisper (ACM FAccT 2024)** — Whisper hallucinations concentrate in long non-vocal/silent regions as phrase repetition. https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.08021
- **Barański et al. (ICASSP 2025)** — Induces Whisper hallucinations on non-speech; a recurring finite "bag of hallucinations" covers most cases; post-hoc guard. https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.11378
- **Frieske & Shi (2024)** — ASR hallucinations are fluent, probable, low-entropy outputs WER cannot separate. https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.01572
- **Sato et al. (Interspeech 2021, "Should We Always Separate?")** — Separators inject artifacts hurting ASR below a crossover; SIR/SNR-gated router. https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.00949
- **Huo et al. (2025)** — Token-level Whisper confidence stable then breaks sharply below ~-5 to -10 dB SNR; overconfident wrong tokens 10-20%. https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.07195

## 4. Honest novelty assessment

**Already established (cite, don't claim):** confident/high-`avg_logprob` hallucinations (Aparin); localization to decoder heads / rank-collapse attractor (Calm-Whisper, Viakhirev); silence/OOD repetition loops (Careless Whisper, Barański, Whisper-CD); per-token logprob-trajectory detection (Corpataux); compression-ratio is a late post-emission signal; separation hurts below a crossover (Sato 2021); post-hoc bag-of-hallucinations filtering (Barański); LLM GER over-corrects on OOD.

**Genuinely new in our direction (defensible):**
1. **Token-id repetition lock-in as a causal trip-wire** — no located work uses token-id repetition at this granularity as an *early* detector. Sharpest novel signal.
2. **Quantifying the offline-compression-ratio router's gain decay under causal prefix forcing** as a separation-tax deployment analysis. Untouched niche.
3. **The specific instantiation**: oracle-separated low-overlap tracks (long silent gaps) → Whisper repetition catastrophe (token 7322 ×224) with `no_speech_prob`≈0.82 + `avg_logprob`≈-0.065 + `compression_ratio`≈37 — a concrete, reproducible mechanism case study. The combination + separation-tax framing is ours; the ingredients are not.
4. Coupling the internal-state detector to the risk-aware router for the overlap-aware ASR pipeline as a deployable artifact.

**To soften:** the strongest form of "causal deployment loses most gain" is refuted by Whisper-CD (causal token-level intervention keeps gain). Reframe: *output-metric gating* loses gain causally; *decode-time/internal-state intervention* need not.

## 5. Framing refinements adopted in FINDINGS

1. Lead with the unoccupied signal (token-id lock-in), not the established mechanism.
2. Scope (c) to output-metric gating; concede Whisper-CD as complementary decode-time intervention.
3. Cite Corpataux + Aparin as bedrock for (b); our delta = token-id granularity + causal/prefix deployment.
4. Differentiate from Sato 2021: Whisper-specific hallucination tail, ratio-continuous, reference-free.
5. Validate-not-assume portability (Waldendorf): probe is Whisper-tiny-specific.
6. Address the `no_speech_prob` discrepancy head-on (our 0.82 high vs Aparin's often-low) as a regime refinement — high `no_speech_prob` may be specific to near-total-silence + repetition-loop inputs.
